About Nature

 

Nature refers to the interaction between the physical world and the life within it like animals, birds, trees, plants, and humans. It also includes non-living things like landscape, water, and others. The surroundings we live in, the food we eat, the water we drink and the air we breathe in are all part of nature. Nature is the primary source of nutrition and nourishment for all living creatures on the earth. Water and oxygen are the essential things for all, and nature has provided us with plenty of both.
